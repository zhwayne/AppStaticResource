Privacy Policy

Product Name: Payday

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Payday collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Payday does not proactively collect, upload, or store your content data on developer-owned servers.

Your data is mainly stored on your own device and/or synced through your own iCloud account.

2. How We Use Information

Payday does not use your content data for advertising or unrelated purposes.

We rely on iCloud only to provide core sync functionality where necessary.

3. iCloud and Third-Party Services

Some features in Payday depend on Apple iCloud services. Apple services are governed by Apple’s own terms and privacy policies.

  • Your data may sync across devices through your Apple account.
  • Service availability/performance may depend on Apple and network conditions.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not upload your app content to developer-owned servers unless legally required.
  • We do not use your content for ad targeting.

5. Data Security

We make reasonable efforts to protect your information. However, no internet or cloud environment can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You can choose whether to use Payday and whether to enable iCloud sync in your device settings.

7. Children

If you are under legal age, please use the app under guardian supervision.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Payday after updates means you accept the revised policy.
